Overall Growth

 

Looking back on the past year, I see that I have grown as a designer and as a person. Since the beginning of the year, I have grown more confident in what I do. I think this is very important, because when I am confident, people will see me as more reliable and trustworthy.

 

Half a year ago I wrote a Personal Development Plan (download) stating the goals I wanted to achieve. Looking back on this I notice that I have achieved these goals in more or lesser degrees. What was apparent was that I had forgotten some goals, until I later had to reflect on them, but still achieved them because I wanted to learn it, for example making a good poster without a lot of effort or conducting user tests. This made me happy, since this means that this were not only goals I had written down, but that I also had the intrinsic motivation to do them.

 

A goal I did not achieve was making a business plan, since I have only checked one. I did, however, grow in aspects of business and entrepreneurship that I did not know existed. I think this is valuable since I now know business and entrepreneurship is more interesting and broader than I originally thought.
