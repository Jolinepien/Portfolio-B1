Project 1

Future

 

In this part I will discuss my plans, goals and activities for the future. I will also discuss how this fits my vision and development.

 

In the upcoming semester and year I want to develop more competencies close to my identity and vision. I am planning on doing the Energy Transition honours track. This will help me to understand sustainable innovations more.

 

In the first quartile of next year I want to make sure my calculus skills will not sink. I will do this by becoming a calculus tutor. This will also help me with my professional skills, since I will need to work in a small team and prepare and present the class.

 

Next year, I will follow a USE learning trajectory. I have chosen to do New Product Development and Marketing. I hope this will help me to develop the competence area business and entrepreneurship and user and society. These are important for me, because this way I can make the impact on society I want.

 

The elective I want to do next year is Creative Mechanical Engineering. Here I will learn material characteristics  and ways to connect parts. This will help to know what material to use in a way that the design will have the desired characteristics, and it might also help to make product more sustainable.

 

Finally, I will do a project next year. I do not yet know what project I will do, since we are not officially divided yet, but I hope to do a project within healthcare. I want this, since I think this will be a good place to start when I want to empower people.

 

Isabelle Frens, 2016
