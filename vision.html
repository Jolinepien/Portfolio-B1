Project 1

Vision

 

In this part I will talk about my Vision on Society and Vision on the role of the Designer. Here I discuss how I think society should change to better and how I think me as designer can contribute to this.

 

 

Vision on Society

I believe we as a society should work together to make efforts to preserve the world as it is (or was). The emissions of greenhouse gases are higher than ever. This should be the time to do something.

 

Furthermore, I believe everyone should have the same opportunities, no matter the socio-demographic characteristics. In our current society there are too much inequalities between races, religions, genders, but also between abled and disabled people.

 

 

Vision on the role of the Designer

As a designer I want to contribute to this society to make  products and services that are environment friendly. I know it is not (yet) possible to design without environmental costs, but I would like to come as close as possible.

 

Moreover, I would also like to design products and services that are affordable for less privileged people.
