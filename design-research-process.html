Design Research Process

 

During last year I have learnt a lot in the design research process. This is the process of going back and forth between making design choices and doing research for future design choices.

 

In my project 1 I have experienced this design process very much. In the beginning of the project we did not know what we would make, so we did not know what we should know. This is why we made choices without having extensive knowledge about the subject. In the time line next to this text, the left side is research and the right side is design development and choices. This makes clear how research stayed a part or our design process.

 

The design research process is important, because I will have to make a lot of choices based on information I do not have. In the process I will have to except that I will have to keep doing research. This is something valuable I have learnt during project 1.
