Identity & Vision

 

It is important to have a personal identity and vision, since this is what will make me stand out from other designers. To develop this I had to do some soul searching in the last year.

 

A development in my vision is that I notice that I think sustainability is more important than I thought at the beginning of the year. This is why I moved it to the top of my vision. This is probably due to various events I have visited in the past year, for example, the March for Science (where I helped with PR).

 

In my identity I have noticed that I find the technology and mechanics behind products more interesting than user sensitivity. This does not mean I do not care about it, but my focus is different than I expected it to be at the beginning of the year.
