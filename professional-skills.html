Project 1

Professional Skills

 

Professional skills are important for a designer, since I have to be able to work in a team and pitch my work to possible employers. During the last year I had a lot of practice with professional skills.

 

For most courses I had to work in small teams, do presentations and write reports. This helped by understanding what desired behavior is and what is not in a group mate. This way I adjusted my own behavior to be better to work with. For example, I share a lot more what I am doing and what I will do for the project and when it will be done. This way my group mates will know what to expect. Furthermore, I have become a lot more confident in English. This makes me less nervous for presenting and pitching. This has made my writing style better as well.

 

During workshops I have been handed multiple tips to improve several professional skills. Being aware of these tricks helps to analyze, for example, my pitch and how to improve it to make it better suitable for the audience. This will be crucial when I am pitching for a possible employer.

 

 

In collaboration with Wouter Leeuw, 2017
