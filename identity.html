Project 1

Identity

 

In this part I will describe my identity. First I will explain what kind of designer I want to be and then what kind of designer I am now and what I am still lacking. How I plan on achieving my goals will be briefly discussed in Future.

 

Desired Identity

As a designer I want to know how to produce a sustainable and affordable product. These are not two aspects that are brought together often. To do this I need to what resources I can use and what characteristics are for certain materials.

 

Another important aspect is that I want to be able to empathize with the user. I want this, since I would like to design with people with disabilities or little to no privilege. I am, on the other hand, completely able and have a lot of privilege. I need to understand the lives of the people from this user group, before I can help them to improve their lives with my designs.

 

Finally, I want to be able to sell and market my products myself, or at least have some sort of insight on how to do this. This is important, since my products will not have an impact in society, if nobody knows about it.

 

 

Current Identity

I have already learnt a lot about understanding the user from the courses Intercultural Design, which tackles cultural issues and differences, and User-Centred Design, which is a course about user research to understand the user. However, I am not an expert in this field yet.

 

On the other hand, I have not learnt a lot about sustainability methods. I have not learnt a lot in the aspect of material characteristics as well. This is something I need to develop in the coming two years.

 

I have a lot to learn in the world of product marketing as well. I have only done one business course yet,which that focused more on the internal part of business.
